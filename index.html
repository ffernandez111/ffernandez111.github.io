About Me

Francisco Fernandez has been a resident of Stamford, Connecticut for the last 25 years. During the early years of Francisco's career, he was a part of the pharmaceutical industry for 7 years working as a compounding and lead pharmacy technician with organizations like Bryce Laboratories and Stamford Hospital. After a career change a few years back, Franciso currently finds himself in the automotive field working as an automotive diagnostic technician. Since Francisco has always had a passion for technology and vehicles, the automotive industry turned out to be a great career choice due to the complexity and intensified pace of the work and the technological advancements in modern day vehicles.

Over the next few months, Francisco will be pursuing a new goal and new skills in the field of web development. Recently having enrolled and currently attending the UCONN School of Engineering Coding Bootcamp, he now plans to start a career in the world of coding. His goal is to obtain a position as a back end developer and to build on his skills and to become a major asset to a company one day.
